   "Make it more photorealistic, professional product photography style"

   by clicking the attachment/image icon in ChatGPT.

   add to your prompt: "No text, no watermarks, no labels"

5. Download images in the highest quality available (JPG format).

6. Recommended: compress images using https://squoosh.app before
   adding to the project (keep file size under 200KB each for
   best page load speed).
