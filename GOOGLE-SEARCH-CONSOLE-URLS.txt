STEP BY STEP — How to Request Indexing
================================================================

1. Open: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Make sure you are on the correct property: gk2switchgear.com
3. Click "URL Inspection" in the left sidebar
4. Paste a URL from the list above into the search bar at top
5. Press Enter and wait for Google to check it
6. Click the button "REQUEST INDEXING"
7. Wait for confirmation message
8. Go back and repeat for the next URL

NOTE: Google allows limited indexing requests per day.
      If you hit the limit, continue the next day.
      Start with HIGH priority URLs first.
